## The Games

**Trumple** — *Bid bold, play smart.* Our original trick-taking game with unique bidding, dynamic trump, and precision scoring. [How to play →](how-to-play-trumple)

**Hearts** — *Dodge the queen.* The classic penalty-avoidance game. Pass cards, avoid hearts, and dodge the Queen of Spades. [How to play →](how-to-play-hearts)

**Spades** — *Bid with your partner.* Partnership trick-taking where spades are always trump. Manage your bags and cover your partner. [How to play →](how-to-play-spades)

**Tarneeb** — *Partner up, call trump.* A popular Middle Eastern partnership game. Win the bid, choose your trump suit, lead your team. [How to play →](how-to-play-tarneeb)

**Big 2** — *Be first to shed.* A shedding game — be first to empty your hand with singles, pairs, and powerful 5-card combos. [How to play →](how-to-play-big2)

## How to Play

**Quick Play** — Play instantly against AI bots. No account needed, works offline.

**Play with Friends** — Create a private lobby, share the room code, and invite friends. Add bots to fill empty seats.

**Find Match** — Join the matchmaking queue and get paired with other players automatically.

---

## Pricing

Trumple (the game) is **free forever** — full offline play against AI bots, no limits, no catches.

**Game Unlocks** — Hearts, Spades, Tarneeb, and Big 2 are one-time purchases. Buy once, own forever.

**Trumple+** — Monthly or annual subscription for online multiplayer, private lobbies, matchmaking, voice chat, and exclusive cosmetics.

Everything is **free during the beta** period. [Full pricing details →](pricing)
