# Trumple 2.0 Beta

Welcome to the Trumple 2.0 beta! We've added four new card games, online matchmaking, and a fresh new look — and during the beta, **everything is completely free**.

---

## What's New in 2.0

- **4 new card games** — Hearts, Spades, Tarneeb, and Big 2 join the lineup alongside the original Trumple
- **Online matchmaking** — Find opponents automatically with the Find Match feature
- **Smarter AI bots** — Improved bot logic across all game types for a better offline experience
- **New UI** — Refreshed visuals, smoother animations, and a more polished experience

---

## Beta Period

Trumple is currently in **free open beta**.

During the beta:
- All 5 game types are **fully unlocked** — no purchases needed
- Online multiplayer is **free for everyone** — no subscription needed
- Everything works — play offline or online, with friends or bots

When the beta ends, the app will transition to its regular pricing model. [See pricing details →]({{ '/pricing' | relative_url }})
