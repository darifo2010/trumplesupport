# How to Play

Trumple features five card games, each for 4 players. Choose your favourite and play offline against AI bots or online with friends.

---

## The Games

### [Trumple]({{ '/how-to-play-trumple' | relative_url }})
*Bid bold, play smart*

Our original trick-taking game with unique bidding mechanics, dynamic trump selection, and precision scoring. Free forever.

### [Hearts]({{ '/how-to-play-hearts' | relative_url }})
*Dodge the queen*

The classic penalty-avoidance card game. Pass cards, avoid hearts, and whatever you do — don't take the Queen of Spades.

### [Spades]({{ '/how-to-play-spades' | relative_url }})
*Bid with your partner*

A partnership trick-taking game where spades are always trump. Bid wisely, watch your bags, and work with your partner.

### [Tarneeb]({{ '/how-to-play-tarneeb' | relative_url }})
*Partner up, call trump*

A Middle Eastern partnership card game. Win the bid, choose your trump suit, and lead your team to victory.

### [Big 2]({{ '/how-to-play-big2' | relative_url }})
*Be first to shed*

A shedding game — be the first to empty your hand. Play singles, pairs, triples, or powerful 5-card combinations.

---

## Game Modes

**Quick Play** — Play instantly against AI bots. No account needed, works offline.

**Play with Friends** — Create a private lobby, share the room code, and invite friends. Add bots to fill empty seats.

**Find Match** — Join the matchmaking queue and get paired with other players automatically.
