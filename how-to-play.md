# How to Play

Trumple has five different card games inside it. Every game is played with 4 players — you and three others. Don't have enough friends around? No problem — the app fills empty seats with smart AI bots.

---

## Pick a Game

### [Trumple]({{ '/how-to-play-trumple' | relative_url }})
*Bid bold, play smart*

Pick cards from your hand to make a bet on how many tricks you'll win. Get it exactly right to score big. Free forever.

### [Hearts]({{ '/how-to-play-hearts' | relative_url }})
*Dodge the queen*

Try NOT to win certain cards. Hearts and the Queen of Spades are bad news — the player with the fewest penalty points wins.

### [Spades]({{ '/how-to-play-spades' | relative_url }})
*Bid with your partner*

Team up with the player across from you. Guess how many tricks you'll win together, then make it happen. Spades are the most powerful cards.

### [Tarneeb]({{ '/how-to-play-tarneeb' | relative_url }})
*Partner up, call trump*

A Middle Eastern team game. Win the bidding war, pick the strongest suit, and lead your team to victory.

### [Big 2]({{ '/how-to-play-big2' | relative_url }})
*Race to empty your hand*

Get rid of all your cards before anyone else. Play singles, pairs, or powerful combos — the first player with no cards left wins.

---

## Three Ways to Play

**Quick Play** — Jump straight in against AI bots. Works offline, no account needed. Great for learning.

**Play with Friends** — Create a private room, share the 6-letter code with friends, and play together. Add bots to fill empty seats.

**Find Match** — Get matched with other real players automatically.
