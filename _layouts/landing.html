The Studio

What I believe in

These aren't marketing lines — they're the reason I build games.

🚫

No ads. Ever.

We will never show ads in any of our games. Your screen is for playing, not watching.

🎲

No pay-to-win

Purchases unlock content, never gameplay advantages. Skill wins games, not spending.

🔒

Privacy first

No tracking SDKs, no analytics harvesting, no selling data. We collect only what the game needs.

💰

Transparent pricing

You know exactly what you're paying for. Buy once, own forever. Subscriptions only for services that cost me to run.

🚀

Always maintained

These are passion projects, not abandonware. Regular updates, smarter AI, new features — every game keeps getting better.

💖

Built with heart

Every game here is something I've always wanted to exist. If I wouldn't play it myself, I wouldn't ship it.
