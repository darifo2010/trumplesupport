Why Trumple?

Not your ordinary card game

Trumple reinvents the trick-taking genre with strategic bidding, dynamic trump selection, and a unique scoring system that rewards precision.

♠

Strategic Bidding

Choose which cards to bid — they reveal your strategy but stay in your hand for play. Same-suit bids set the trump.

🎭

13 Tricks, Every Card

No discards, no throwaways. All 13 cards matter across 13 tricks. Nail your bid for maximum points.

⚡

Real-Time Multiplayer

Play with friends using room codes or fill seats with smart AI bots. Auto-pause on disconnect, auto-rejoin on return.

🎯

Precision Scoring

Hit your bid exactly for big points. Go over or under and pay the price. Zero-bid gambles can pay off — or backfire.

Voice Chat

Talk trash and strategize with walkie-talkie style voice streaming. Hold to speak, release to listen.

Achievements & Stats

Track your win rate, bid accuracy, and streaks. Unlock Game Center achievements as you master the game.
