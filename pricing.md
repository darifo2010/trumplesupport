# Pricing

Trumple is built on a simple promise: **no ads, no gambling, no pay-to-win.** Just pure card game fun.

---

## Free Forever

**Trumple** — our original trick-taking game — is completely free. Play offline against AI bots anytime, no account needed. Always free, no catches.

---

## Game Type Unlocks

Unlock additional card games with a one-time purchase. **Buy once, own forever.**

- Hearts
- Spades
- Tarneeb
- Big 2

Each unlock gives you full offline play against AI bots. Our bot AI is continuously improved — your offline experience keeps getting better with every update. Prices are shown in the app in your local currency.

---

## Trumple+ Subscription

Take your games online with **Trumple+**.

Available as a monthly or annual plan. Prices are shown in the app in your local currency.

### What's Included

- **Online multiplayer** — Play all your owned game types with real people
- **Private lobbies** — Create rooms and invite friends with a room code
- **Matchmaking** — Get paired with other players automatically
- **Exclusive avatar glow rings** — Stand out at the table with animated ring effects around your avatar
- **Exclusive card backs** — Customise the back of your cards with premium designs
- **Voice chat** — Talk with your tablemates during games *(coming soon)*

Trumple+ unlocks online play for game types you own. Trumple is always owned (it's free), so a subscription alone gets you Trumple online. For other games, you need both the game unlock and the subscription.

---

## Our Promise

- **No ads.** Ever. We will never show ads in Trumple.
- **No gambling.** No real money stakes, no loot boxes, no gacha mechanics.
- **No pay-to-win.** Purchases unlock content — they never give gameplay advantages.
- **Buy once, own forever.** Game unlocks are permanent one-time purchases.
- **Smarter bots, always.** We continuously improve our AI — your offline games keep getting better.

---

## Beta Period

During the free open beta, **everything is free** — all game types and online play are fully unlocked. Pricing takes effect when the beta ends.
